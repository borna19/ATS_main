About Our ATS System

An intelligent AI-powered solution to evaluate resumes based on job requirements and help recruiters shortlist the most relevant candidates.

✨ What Our ATS Can Do

AI-Powered Resume Analysis

Automatically scans each resume and identifies keywords, skills, experience level, and job-specific requirements using advanced.

ATS Score Calculation

Computes ATS match score using keyword relevance, skill alignment, formatting, and job description similarity.

Visual Performance Insights

Provides charts comparing matched vs missing keywords, enabling HR to make informed hiring decisions quickly.

⚙️ How the System Works

1. Upload Resume

Upload one or multiple resumes in PDF or DOCX format. Strengths, eligibility, and more.

2. AI Keyword Extraction

Extracts important skills, technical terms, tools, and experience indicators.

3. ATS Scoring Engine

Compares extracted keywords with the job description and computes an accuracy score.

4. Final Report

Generates detailed candidate ranking, keyword insights, and selection recommendations.
